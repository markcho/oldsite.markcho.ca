About Me

Please see my resume for the projects I worked on during my work terms.

When I was in elementary school, my passion for games led me to learn to program in VBScript and AutoIt to develop extensions for my favourite games. Besides gaming, my hobbies also include playing sports, especially hockey and snowboarding during the winter season.

About Me
Hello, my name is Mark Cho and I'm currently in my 2nd year (2B) of Software Engineering at the University of Waterloo.

During the last 2 years (2010-2011), I spent 2 coop work terms (8 months) at Aria Solutions Inc. in Calgary. While I was at Aria Solutions, some of the major projects I worked on includes developing a custom script executor extension for two different products using C# and JavaScript and developing an automated test framework and a load test application, both products in C#. I'm also familiar with C/C++, as I've done most of my coursework at school and personal projects using these languages.

During my spare time, I enjoy playing video games such as Starcraft II and the NHL series. Through computer games, I've done some projects using VBScript and AutoIt to develop extensions and mods. Besides gaming, my hobbies also include playing sports, especially hockey and snowboarding during the winter season.
